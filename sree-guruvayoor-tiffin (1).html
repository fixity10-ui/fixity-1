Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

Born from the sacred culinary traditions of Guruvayoor, our tiffin house was founded on a simple belief: honest food, cooked with devotion, feeds both body and soul.

From a single outlet in Thrissur to three thriving branches, our recipes remain unchanged — passed down with the same love and precision that made our first customers return day after day.

  • 100% vegetarian menu, certified and lovingly maintained
  • Fresh ingredients sourced from local Kerala farmers daily
  • Open from 6 AM — because mornings deserve the best
  • Ample free parking and clean, welcoming premises
  • LGBTQ+ friendly space — everyone is welcome at our table
